## 1. RNA-Seq Profiling Reveals Transcriptional Changes in Phosphorylation-Site Mutants of Brg1.

**MeSH:** DNA Helicases, Transcription Factors, Phosphorylation, Nuclear Proteins, Humans, Mutation, Cell Line, Tumor, RNA-Seq, Apoptosis, Gene Expression Regulation, Neoplastic, Female, Transcription, Genetic, Ovarian Neoplasms

### 摘要

Brg1 is a core ATPase subunit of the SWItch/Sucrose Non-Fermentable (SWI/SNF) chromatin-remodeling complex that regulates DNA accessibility for RNA polymerase II (Pol II), transcription factors, and DNA repair enzymes. Phosphoproteomic profiling of highly malignant ovarian clear cell carcinoma (OCCC) cell lines revealed reduced levels of several SWI/SNF components and decreased Brg1 phosphorylation within its histone-binding region. Prior work showed that phosphorylation-mimic and phosphorylation-deficient mutants alter chromatin-silencing factors, producing chromatin condensation and decondensation, respectively. Here, we performed RNA-seq in Brg1-deficient JHOC-5 cells and in isogenic cells expressing Brg1-WT, phosphorylation-mimic brg1-S1452D, or phosphorylation-deficient brg1-S1452A. PCA and hierarchical clustering separated brg1-S1452A from the other lines, indicating a strong transcriptional impact of Ser1452 dephosphorylation. JHOC-5 and brg1-S1452A shared altered expression of Pol II-promoter-regulated genes. Apoptosis genes (BCL2, FGFR2, ZC3H12A, and NFKBIA) showed reciprocal expression between brg1-S1452D and brg1-S1452A, consistent with increased and decreased apoptosis, respectively. Genes linked to cell adhesion/migration and neuronal development also differed, accompanied by reduced cell circularity in brg1-S1452A. Overall, Brg1 phosphorylation at Ser1452 shapes SWI/SNF-mediated chromatin remodeling to regulate transcriptional programs controlling apoptosis and cell morphology.

### 内容概述

Brg1是SWItch/Sucrose Non-Fermentable（SWI/SNF）染色质重塑复合物的核心ATP酶亚基，可调控RNA聚合酶II（Pol II）、转录因子及DNA修复酶对DNA的可及性。对高侵袭性卵巢透明细胞癌（OCCC）细胞系的磷酸化蛋白质组学分析显示，多个SWI/SNF组分水平降低，且其组蛋白结合区域内的Brg1磷酸化程度下降。既往研究表明，磷酸化模拟突变体与磷酸化缺陷突变体可分别通过改变染色质沉默因子诱导染色质凝集与解凝集。本研究对Brg1缺陷型JHOC-5细胞、表达野生型Brg1（Brg1-WT）、磷酸化模拟突变体Brg1-S1452D或磷酸化缺陷突变体Brg1-S1452A的同基因细胞进行了RNA测序。主成分分析与层级聚类将Brg1-S1452A与其他细胞系分离，提示Ser1452去磷酸化具有显著转录效应。JHOC-5细胞与Brg1-S1452A共同表现出Pol II启动子调控基因的表达改变。凋亡相关基因（BCL2、FGFR2、ZC3H12A、NFKBIA）在Brg1-S1452D与Brg1-S1452A中呈现相反表达模式，分别对应凋亡活性增强与减弱。细胞黏附/迁移及神经元发育相关基因的表达亦存在差异，同时Brg1-S1452A细胞圆形度降低。综上，Brg1在Ser1452位点的磷酸化通过塑造SWI/SNF介导的染色质重塑，调控控制凋亡与细胞形态的转录程序。

## 2. Automated imaging workflow for FISH-stained tissue microarrays enables assessment of FGFR2 translocations in a large intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma patient cohort.

| 字段 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| **作者** | Isis Lozzi; Jan Schmoranzer; Alexander Arnold; Anja Reutzel-Selke; Felix Krenzien; Igor M Sauer; Johann Pratschke; Wenzel Schöning |
| **期刊** | Experimental and molecular pathology |
| **发表日期** | 2026 Jul 17 |
| **DOI** | `10.1016/j.yexmp.2026.105067` |
| **PMID** | `42468416` |
| **PubMed** | [查看原文](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42468416/) |

**关键词:** Biliary tract cancer, Break-apart probe, Digital pathology, Fluorescence in situ hybridization, Molecular diagnostics

### 摘要

**BACKGROUND:** Fibroblast growth factor receptor-2 (FGFR2) translocations are frequently observed in intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma (iCCA) and represent actionable therapeutic targets. While fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH) is commonly used for their detection, its applicability to large cohorts, particularly on tissue microarrays (TMAs), is limited by time-consuming and error-prone manual scoring due to core disorientation. We therefore sought to develop an automated imaging workflow to enable efficient and scalable assessment of FGFR2 translocations in FISH-stained TMAs.

**METHODS:** Tumor samples from 86 iCCA patients were obtained to construct TMAs, which underwent FISH staining for the detection of FGFR2 translocations. Slides were imaged with an automated inverted wide-field fluorescence microscope. First, a whole-slide overview scan at low resolution was acquired to identify TMA cores. Subsequently, a high-resolution automated scan was performed across the identified regions of interest (ROI) using a custom-built general analysis script with optimized parameters for single-cell analysis. Images were stored with positional metadata for downstream evaluation.

**RESULTS:** Of 86 cases, 85 yielded images with sufficient quality for FGFR2 translocation analysis. Interphase FISH evaluation of the generated images revealed that 8.2% (7/85) of patients tested positive, consistent with reported frequencies.

**CONCLUSIONS:** We developed an automated imaging workflow for high-throughput acquisition and archiving of FISH-stained TMAs for FGFR2 translocation assessment in iCCA. By enabling standardized digital imaging, precise core identification, and permanent image preservation, the workflow facilitates efficient and reproducible review of FISH images from large TMA cohorts in both diagnostic and translational research settings. It is readily adaptable to other FISH-based assays and staining protocols, extending its utility beyond FGFR2 testing.

### 内容概述

背景：成纤维细胞生长因子受体2（FGFR2）易位在肝内胆管癌（iCCA）中频繁出现，是可行的治疗靶点。荧光原位杂交（FISH）虽常用于检测该突变，但由于组织芯片（TMAs）中核心方向错乱导致人工评分耗时且易出错，限制了其在大型队列（尤其基于TMAs）中的应用。因此，我们旨在开发一种自动化成像流程，以实现FISH染色的TMA中FGFR2易位的高效、可规模化评估。

方法：采集86例iCCA患者的肿瘤样本构建TMA，并进行FISH染色以检测FGFR2易位。使用自动倒置宽场荧光显微镜对切片进行成像。首先通过低分辨率全切片概览扫描识别TMA核心，随后采用自定义通用分析脚本（针对单细胞分析优化参数）在识别出的感兴趣区域（ROI）进行高分辨率自动扫描。图像连同位置元数据一并保存，用于后续评估。

结果：86例样本中，85例获得足以进行FGFR2易位分析的图像。对生成图像的间期FISH评估显示，8.2%（7/85）的患者呈阳性，与文献报道的发生率一致。

结论：我们开发了一套自动化成像流程，用于高通量获取和归档FISH染色的TMA，以评估iCCA中的FGFR2易位。该流程通过实现标准化数字成像、精准核心识别及永久图像保存，便于在诊断和转化研究中高效、可重复地审阅大规模TMA队列的FISH图像。该方法可灵活适用于其他基于FISH的检测方法和染色方案，从而超越FGFR2检测的局限性。
