## 1. Dual targeting of PI3K and FGFR or CDK4/6 reveals synergistic responses in HPV-positive and HPV-negative oropharyngeal cancer spheroids.

### 摘要

Human papillomavirus-positive (HPV+) oropharyngeal squamous cell carcinoma (OPSCC) generally has a better prognosis than HPV-negative (HPV-) OPSCC. Still, chemoradiotherapy does not cure all patients; therefore, novel treatments are needed. In monolayers of HPV+ / - OPSCC CU-OP cell lines, we previously demonstrated that phosphoinositide 3-kinase (PI3K), fibroblast growth factor receptor (FGFR), and cyclin-dependent kinase 4/6 (CDK4/6) inhibitors reduced viability and had synergistic effects when combined. Here, to better simulate the in vivo state, the effects of these inhibitors and cytostatics were assessed in spheroids of HPV+ / - CU-OP cell lines. All spheroids were treated with single administrations of PI3K (BYL719), FGFR (JNJ-42756493), or CDK4/6 (PD-0332991) inhibitors or cisplatin or docetaxel, as well as with combinations of BYL719 with all named drugs. All inhibitors reduced cell viability and growth in all spheroids, especially in CU-OP-2, while the effects of cisplatin and docetaxel were more limited. Combining BYL719 with all other drugs resulted in additive or synergistic effects in CU-OP-2, while corresponding treatments generally required higher doses to reach similar effects in the other cell lines. Interestingly, the high drug sensitivity of CU-OP-2 differed from the observations made in earlier monolayer studies, where CU-OP-20 was the most sensitive cell line, highlighting the importance of using spheroids in drug screenings.

### 内容概述

人乳头瘤病毒阳性（HPV+）口咽鳞状细胞癌（OPSCC）通常比HPV阴性（HPV-）OPSCC预后更好。然而，放化疗并不能治愈所有患者，因此需要开发新型治疗方法。在HPV+/ - OPSCC的CU-OP细胞系单层培养中，我们先前发现磷脂酰肌醇3-激酶（PI3K）、成纤维细胞生长因子受体（FGFR）和细胞周期蛋白依赖性激酶4/6（CDK4/6）抑制剂能降低细胞活力，且联用时具有协同效应。为更好模拟体内状态，本研究在HPV+/ - CU-OP细胞系球状体中评估了这些抑制剂和细胞抑制剂的作用。所有球状体分别接受单次给药：PI3K抑制剂（BYL719）、FGFR抑制剂（JNJ-42756493）、CDK4/6抑制剂（PD-0332991）、顺铂或多西他赛，以及BYL719与上述所有药物的联合治疗。所有抑制剂均降低了各球状体的细胞活力和生长，尤其在CU-OP-2中效果显著，而顺铂和多西他赛的作用较为有限。将BYL719与其他药物联用在CU-OP-2中产生相加或协同效应，而其他细胞系通常需要更高剂量才能达到类似效果。值得注意的是，CU-OP-2的高药物敏感性与早期单层研究中观察到的结果不同（此前CU-OP-20是最敏感细胞系），凸显了在药物筛选中使用球状体的重要性。

## 2. Temporal Dynamics of Extracellular Signal-Regulated Kinase Phosphorylation and Associated Signaling Genes During Embryonic Development in Bombyx mori.

**关键词:** FGF, Jeb/Alk, MAPK, RTK, TGF‐β, embryonic diapause, huckebein, pointed

**MeSH:** Animals, Bombyx, Phosphorylation, Signal Transduction, Gene Expression Regulation, Developmental, Extracellular Signal-Regulated MAP Kinases, Embryonic Development, Insect Proteins, Diapause, Insect

### 摘要

This study investigated the temporal dynamics of extracellular signal-regulated kinase (ERK) phosphorylation, together with the expression profiles of its putative upstream ligands and downstream target genes, during embryonic development in Bombyx mori. The results revealed distinct ERK phosphorylation dynamics: significantly higher levels of phosphorylated ERK were observed between Days 1 and 6 after oviposition in developing eggs (non-diapause and HCl-treated eggs), whereas diapause eggs exhibited low levels. The temporal expression patterns of genes in the prothoracicotropic hormone (PTTH)/torso and epidermal growth factor (EGF) signaling pathways-spitz (spi), rhomboid (rho), and Egf receptor (Egfr)-did not differ between diapause and HCl-treated eggs. In contrast, the EGF pathway gene vein (vn) and genes in the transforming growth factor (TGF)-β signaling pathway-including the ligand myoglianin (myo) and its receptor babo-displayed distinct temporal expression patterns. Similarly, Jelly belly (Jeb)/anaplastic lymphoma kinase (Alk), fibroblast growth factor (Fgf)/Fgf receptor (Fgfr), neurotrophin (NT), and Toll-receptor ligands (spätzle family members (spz2, spz4, and spz5)) showed differential expression, with levels significantly elevated during the middle and late stages of embryonic development in HCl-treated eggs. Non-diapause and chilled eggs also exhibited increasing expression patterns of these genes, similar to those observed in HCl-treated eggs. Additionally, ERK target genes pointed and huckebein (hkb) showed higher and broader expression peaks in developing eggs compared to diapause eggs. This study provides a comprehensive analysis of the transcriptional regulation of upstream and downstream ERK signaling components during embryonic development and reveals new insights into the mechanisms underlying embryonic diapause.

### 内容概述

本研究调查了家蚕（Bombyx mori）胚胎发育过程中胞外信号调节激酶（ERK）磷酸化的时间动态，以及其推测上游配体和下游靶基因的表达谱。结果显示，ERK磷酸化呈现显著动态变化：在产卵后第1至第6天的发育卵（非滞育卵和盐酸处理卵）中，磷酸化ERK水平明显升高，而滞育卵中则维持在低水平。前胸腺激素（PTTH）/ torso和表皮生长因子（EGF）信号通路基因（spitz（spi）、rhomboid（rho）和Egf受体（Egfr））的时间表达模式在滞育卵与盐酸处理卵之间无差异。相反，EGF通路基因vein（vn）以及转化生长因子（TGF）-β信号通路基因（包括配体myoglianin（myo）及其受体babo）则表现出独特的时间表达模式。同样，Jelly belly（Jeb）/间变性淋巴瘤激酶（Alk）、成纤维细胞生长因子（Fgf）/Fgf受体（Fgfr）、神经营养因子（NT）和Toll受体配体（spätzle家族成员spz2、spz4和spz5）的表达也存在差异，在盐酸处理卵的胚胎发育中后期水平显著升高。非滞育卵和冷处理卵也呈现出与盐酸处理卵相似的这些基因表达上升模式。此外，ERK靶基因pointed和huckebein（hkb）在发育卵中的表达峰值高于滞育卵，且表达范围更广。本研究全面解析了胚胎发育过程中ERK信号通路上下游组分的转录调控机制，并为揭示胚胎滞育的调控机制提供了新见解。
