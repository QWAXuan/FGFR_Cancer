## 1. FGFR2 is an oncogenic driver critical for successful human gammaherpesvirus latent infection and oncogenesis.

| 字段 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| **作者** | Nian Ma; Dipayan Bose; Jakob Svoboda; Elise A Chong; Stephen J Schuster; Erle S Robertson |
| **期刊** | Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America |

| **DOI** | `10.1073/pnas.2602413123` |
| **PMID** | `42479845` |

**关键词:** FGFR2, cancer, gammaherpesvirus, kinases, lymphoma

**MeSH:** Humans, Virus Latency, Receptor, Fibroblast Growth Factor, Type 2, Herpesvirus 8, Human, Herpesvirus 4, Human, Animals, STAT3 Transcription Factor, Carcinogenesis, Lymphoma, Antigens, Viral, Epstein-Barr Virus Infections, Latent Infection, Nuclear Proteins, Signal Transduction, Mice

### 摘要

Epstein-Barr virus (EBV) and Kaposi's sarcoma-associated herpesvirus (KSHV) drive multiple aggressive lymphomas, yet effective targeted therapies for these virus-associated malignancies remain limited. Using an unbiased kinome-wide screen combined with analysis of virus-positive patient tumors, we identified fibroblast growth factor receptor 2 (FGFR2) as a selectively activated host kinase in EBV- and KSHV-associated lymphomas. Importantly, FGFR2 is required for efficient establishment of EBV latent infection, and its knockdown markedly impairs the formation of viral latency programs. Viral latency proteins EBV nuclear antigen 2 (EBNA2) and latency-associated nuclear antigen (LANA) recruit STAT3 and RBP-Jκ to the FGFR2 promoter to drive its transcription, enabling efficient establishment of EBV latency and activation of downstream STAT3/AKT signaling. This feed-forward signaling circuit suppresses apoptosis, promotes S-phase progression, and sustains proliferation of infected lymphoma cells. Targeting FGFR2 genetically or pharmacologically using clinically relevant inhibitors markedly suppresses tumor growth in vitro and in vivo. This study identifies FGFR2 as a critical oncogenic driver in EBV and KSHV infections, highlighting its potential as a therapeutic target to inhibit tumor growth and treat associated viral malignancies.

### 内容概述

Epstein-Barr病毒（EBV）和卡波西肉瘤相关疱疹病毒（KSHV）驱动多种侵袭性淋巴瘤，但针对这些病毒相关恶性肿瘤的有效靶向疗法仍然有限。通过无偏激酶组全谱筛选结合病毒阳性患者肿瘤分析，我们鉴定出成纤维细胞生长因子受体2（FGFR2）是EBV和KSHV相关淋巴瘤中选择性激活的宿主激酶。重要的是，FGFR2是EBV有效建立潜伏感染所必需的，其敲低显著损害病毒潜伏程序的建立。病毒潜伏蛋白EBV核抗原2（EBNA2）和潜伏相关核抗原（LANA）将STAT3和RBP-Jκ招募至FGFR2启动子驱动其转录，从而促进EBV潜伏期的有效建立及下游STAT3/AKT信号通路的激活。这种前馈信号环路抑制细胞凋亡、促进S期进程并维持受感染淋巴瘤细胞的增殖。通过基因手段或临床应用相关抑制剂靶向FGFR2，可在体外和体内显著抑制肿瘤生长。本研究将FGFR2鉴定为EBV和KSHV感染中的关键致癌驱动因子，突显其作为抑制肿瘤生长和治疗相关病毒恶性肿瘤的潜在治疗靶点。

## 2. Dynamic Changes in Circulating and Tissue Biomarkers and Therapeutic Response to HER2/PD-1 Dual Blockade in HER2-Positive Gastric Cancer.

### 摘要

**BACKGROUND:** HER2-positive gastric adenocarcinoma demonstrates heterogeneous responses to dual HER2/PD-1 blockade therapy. This study investigated integrated circulating tumor DNA, protein biomarkers, and immune cell profiling for predicting treatment outcomes and monitoring therapeutic resistance.

**METHODS:** This retrospective analysis included 72 patients with HER2-positive gastric or gastroesophageal junction adenocarcinoma receiving anti-HER2 therapy plus PD-1 inhibitors. Serial blood samples underwent circulating tumor DNA sequencing, protein biomarker measurement, and flow cytometric immune cell characterization. Multivariable analysis identified independent predictors and developed an integrated biomarker model for patient stratification.

**RESULTS:** Objective response rate was 40.3% with a median progression-free survival of 11.4 months. Sustained circulating tumor DNA suppression in 24 patients correlated with prolonged survival compared to early rebound patterns (p = 0.048). Co-occurring genomic alterations in EGFR, PIK3CA, MYC, and FGFR2 were detected in ctDNA and were associated with shorter progression-free survival. CD8+ T-cell increases and natural killer cell activity patterns differed between responders and non-responders. Multivariable analysis identified composite immune score (HR 0.58, p = 0.003), tumor stage (HR 2.31, p < 0.001), and performance status as independent predictors. An exploratory integrated biomarker model stratified patients into risk categories of differing outcome, although discrimination was modest (AUC 0.65) and requires prospective validation.

**CONCLUSIONS:** Integrated circulating tumor DNA and immune biomarker monitoring may provide prognostic information for dual HER2/PD-1 blockade therapy. Serial molecular profiling enables early resistance detection and identifies evolutionary patterns that may inform therapeutic strategies, though prospective validation is required.

### 内容概述

背景：HER2阳性胃腺癌对双靶HER2/PD-1阻断治疗呈现异质性应答。本研究探讨整合循环肿瘤DNA、蛋白生物标志物及免疫细胞图谱在预测治疗结局及监测耐药性中的作用。

方法：本回顾性分析纳入72例接受抗HER2联合PD-1抑制剂治疗的HER2阳性胃或胃食管交界处腺癌患者。对系列血样进行循环肿瘤DNA测序、蛋白生物标志物检测及流式细胞术免疫细胞分型。通过多变量分析识别独立预测因子，构建整合生物标志物模型用于患者分层。

结果：客观缓解率为40.3%，中位无进展生存期为11.4个月。24例患者出现持续性循环肿瘤DNA抑制，其生存期较早期反弹模式显著延长（p=0.048）。ctDNA中检测到EGFR、PIK3CA、MYC及FGFR2共存的基因组变异，并与较短的无进展生存期相关。应答者与非应答者的CD8+T细胞增殖及自然杀伤细胞活性模式存在差异。多变量分析确定复合免疫评分（HR 0.58，p=0.003）、肿瘤分期（HR 2.31，p<0.001）及体能状态为独立预测因子。探索性整合生物标志物模型虽将患者分为不同结局风险等级，但区分度有限（AUC 0.65），需前瞻性验证。

结论：整合循环肿瘤DNA与免疫生物标志物监测可为双靶HER2/PD-1阻断治疗提供预后信息。系列分子图谱分析能实现早期耐药识别，揭示可能指导治疗策略的演化模式，但需前瞻性验证。

## 3. Calcified Chondroid Mesenchymal Neoplasm of the Temporomandibular Joint Region: Three Cases Illustrating Recent Developments and Diagnostic Pitfalls.

**关键词:** FGFR1, FGFR2, FN1, PDGFRA, USP8, Calcified chondroid mesenchymal neoplasm, Head and neck, Temporomandibular joint, Tophaceous pseudogout

**MeSH:** Humans, Female, Male, Middle Aged, Retrospective Studies, Diagnosis, Differential, Aged, Adult, Temporomandibular Joint Disorders

### 摘要

**PURPOSE:** Calcified chondroid mesenchymal neoplasm (CCMN) is a recently described and evolving entity that predominantly arises in the distal extremities andtemporomandibular joint (TMJ) region. CCMN is characterized by FN1 and PDGFRA gene fusions. In the TMJ region, most cases previously described as tophaceous pseudogout (i.e., mass-forming calcium pyrophosphate deposition disease) are now being increasingly recognized as CCMN. There is considerable morphologic overlap between CCMN and tophaceous pseudogout (TPG), and the detection of FN1 and PDGFRA gene rearrangements is critical in distinguishing CCMN from TPG. We aim to identify, from our archival records, cases of CCMN that were previously diagnosed as TPG.

**METHODS:** We retrospectively reviewed clinical history and histopathology slides of three cases of TMJ region masses initially diagnosed as TPG, and submitted all three cases for targeted RNA sequencing.

**RESULTS:** Case 1 harbored a canonical FN1::FGFR2 fusion. Case 2 failed targeted RNA sequencing. Case 3 harbored a canonical PDGFRA::USP8 fusion. None of the three cases had clinical history of pseudogout. All three cases were subsequently reclassified as CCMN on review.

**CONCLUSION:** CCMN should be considered as a differential diagnosis in first presentations of 'tophaceous pseudogout' associated with chondroid metaplasia, with targeted molecular testing done as part of the diagnostic process.

### 内容概述

目的：钙化软骨样间叶性肿瘤（CCMN）是一种近期描述且仍在演变的实体，主要发生于四肢远端及颞下颌关节（TMJ）区域。其典型特征为存在FN1和PDGFRA基因融合。在颞下颌关节区域，既往多数被诊断为痛风性假性痛风（即形成肿块的焦磷酸钙沉积病）的病例，现逐渐被识别为CCMN。CCMN与痛风性假性痛风（TPG）在形态学上存在显著重叠，而检测FN1和PDGFRA基因重排是区分二者的关键。本研究旨在通过存档病例，筛选既往诊断为TPG的CCMN病例。

方法：回顾性分析三例初诊为TPG的颞下颌关节区域肿块病例的临床病史及组织病理切片，并将所有三例标本提交进行靶向RNA测序。

结果：病例1存在经典FN1::FGFR2融合；病例2靶向RNA测序失败；病例3存在经典PDGFRA::USP8融合。三例均无假性痛风病史。经复核，所有病例均重新归类为CCMN。

结论：对于首次表现为伴软骨样化生的“痛风性假性痛风”病例，应将CCMN纳入鉴别诊断，并在诊断流程中纳入靶向分子检测。

## 4. Undifferentiated embryonal sarcoma of the liver with a novel MAD1L1::ERG fusion: a 20-year survival case report.

| **PMC ID** | `5026735` |

**关键词:** MAD1L1:ERG fusion, Molecular profiling, Undifferentiated embryonal sarcoma of the liver (UESL)

### 摘要

Undifferentiated embryonal sarcoma of the liver (UESL) is a rare, highly aggressive malignant mesenchymal tumor that predominantly affects children and typically carries a poor prognosis. We report a unique UESL case with long-term survival harboring a novel MAD1L1::ERG gene fusion. A 15-year-old female underwent segmentectomy for a 15-cm hepatic mass, without a precise diagnosis initially. From 2018 to 2025, she experienced multiple recurrences involving the liver, right kidney, and retroperitoneum. Histopathological examination across all recurrences consistently showed classic UESL features: pleomorphic spindle or polygonal cells in a myxoid stroma, frequent mitotic figures, bizarre multinucleated giant cells, and characteristic PAS-positive eosinophilic hyaline globules. Immunohistochemistry (IHC) was positive for vimentin. Targeted DNA next-generation sequencing (NGS) identified 23 genetic alterations, most notably a novel MAD1L1::ERG gene fusion (breakpoint: MAD1L1 Intron 18 and ERG Exon 13) alongside CCND2, FGFR3, MYC, SRC amplifications, and a TP53 mutation. Despite the tumor's highly aggressive behavior, the patient achieved long-term survival through repeated multi-visceral resections and systemic chemotherapy (VAC and IE). This report expands the molecular genetic profile of UESL by identifying a previously undescribed gene rearrangement.

### 内容概述

未分化胚胎性肝肉瘤（UESL）是一种罕见、高度恶性的间叶性肿瘤，主要发生于儿童，通常预后不良。我们报告一例具有长期生存的独特UESL病例，携带新型MAD1L1::ERG基因融合。一名15岁女性患者因肝脏15厘米肿块接受肝段切除术，初始诊断不明确。2018年至2025年间，患者出现多次复发，累及肝脏、右肾及腹膜后区域。所有复发灶的组织病理学检查均显示典型UESL特征：黏液样间质中见多形性梭形或多边形细胞、频繁核分裂象、奇异型多核巨细胞及特征性PAS阳性嗜酸性透明球。免疫组化（IHC）显示波形蛋白阳性。靶向DNA二代测序（NGS）鉴定出23种基因变异，最显著的是新型MAD1L1::ERG基因融合（断点：MAD1L1内含子18与ERG外显子13），同时伴有CCND2、FGFR3、MYC、SRC扩增及TP53突变。尽管肿瘤具有高度侵袭性，但通过反复多脏器切除术及全身化疗（VAC方案和IE方案），患者实现了长期生存。本报告通过发现此前未描述的基因重排，拓展了UESL的分子遗传学图谱。
